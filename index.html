About This Game

Steal Brainrot is a hilarious merge game featuring viral internet memes. Drop brainrot characters onto the board and merge identical ones to create new, more powerful memes. The game combines addictive merge mechanics with the funniest brainrot content from the internet.

How to Play

  • Click or tap to drop brainrot characters onto the board
  • When two identical characters touch, they merge into a higher-level character
  • Plan your drops strategically to create merge chains
  • Unlock all brainrot characters to win!
  • Don't let the board fill up - game over if no moves left

Tips & Tricks

  • Start by placing characters in corners for better control
  • Create merge chains for bonus points
  • Keep space for high-tier characters
  • Plan ahead - don't rush your drops
  • Learn the merge order to optimize your strategy
